For movement, we developed a system that allowed players to move by pulling themselves and the platform they were standing on, utilizing some holographic banisters. Our goal was to enhance immersion while minimizing the risk of motion sickness. We included arrows in the holograms to guide players through the ship, along with adding subtle proximity-to-hand effects to enhance feedback.

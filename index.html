About me

Hello! I'm Jesús Martinez Rodríguez, a passionate game developer specialized in game design and programming.

My passion for games started at a young age when I would create my own custom rules while playing. Video games have broadened my perspective and shaped the way I see the world. Today, I view games as the ultimate form of human communication.

Through my education and years of experience, I've gained a comprehensive understanding of the game development process. While I specialize in Game Design and Gameplay Programming, I've worked across various areas of development. This broad expertise has provided me with a long-term vision, enabling me to design and implement features that seamlessly integrate into the game as a whole while anticipating potential challenges. In my most recent project, this experience allowed me to take on the role of Product Owner, achieving excellent results while managing a multidisciplinary team of up to 20 developers.

In recent projects, I've primarily focused on the 3Cs: Character, Controller, and Camera, with a particular emphasis on character movement mechanics in 3D worlds. I've also contributed to a wider range of gameplay features, including puzzles, interactables, levels, UI, AI, and combat, often taking on the development of quick prototypes. This experience has helped me maintain flexibility in my approach and skills.

I pay close attention to detail, fine-tuning elements that may not always be visible but are definitely felt; especially when it comes to camera systems and input.

I have extensive experience working with popular game engines like Unreal Engine and Unity, with commercially released games in both. I stay up to date with the latest development trends and techniques, frequently participating in game jams to explore new ideas and broaden my knowledge across various genres.
